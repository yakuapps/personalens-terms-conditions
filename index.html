‚ùìFrequently Asked Questions

Is my chat data safe and private?
Yes! PersonaLens uses a unique privacy-first architecture. Your original chat messages are anonymized locally on your device. Only the anonymized data is sent to our AI models for analysis. No chat data is stored on our servers - analysis happens on-the-fly and data is immediately discarded after processing.
How does the share extension work?
After installing PersonaLens, you can export a WhatsApp chat and select "PersonaLens" from the share menu. This automatically imports the chat into the app for analysis - no need to manually find and select files.
What happens to my data during AI analysis?
Your messages are processed locally to remove identifying information like names, phone numbers, and specific details. Only patterns, communication style, and anonymized content are sent to our AI models. The original messages never leave your device.
Which messaging apps does PersonaLens support?
Currently, PersonaLens works with WhatsApp chat exports. We're working on adding support for iMessage, Telegram, and other popular messaging platforms in future updates.
How accurate is the personality analysis?
PersonaLens uses advanced AI models trained on personality psychology and the scientifically-backed Big Five model (OCEAN). Our analysis considers communication patterns, language use, emotional expression, and conversation dynamics to provide meaningful personality insights.
What's the difference between free and premium features?
Free version includes basic personality analysis and compatibility scoring. Premium unlocks advanced insights like predictive relationship analysis, detailed sentiment trends, comprehensive topic analysis, and unlimited AI character art generation.
Why does analysis sometimes take time?
PersonaLens performs sophisticated AI analysis using advanced language models. Processing time depends on chat length and internet connection speed. Longer conversations (1000+ messages) may take 1-3 minutes to analyze completely.
Can I analyze group chats?
Currently, PersonaLens works best with one-on-one conversations for the most accurate personality and compatibility analysis. Group chat analysis is planned for a future update.

üîßTroubleshooting

Share Extension Not Appearing

Solutions:
  • Make sure PersonaLens is fully installed and opened at least once
  • Export chat "Without Media" for smaller file size
  • If still not showing, use direct import method in the app
  • Restart your device if the extension isn't visible

Analysis Takes Too Long

Possible Fixes:
  • Check your internet connection (needed for AI analysis)
  • Try a shorter conversation first to test
  • Close other apps to free up device resources
  • Ensure stable WiFi or cellular connection

Import Error

Common Solutions:
  • Ensure the file is exported as a ZIP (not TXT)
  • Export "Without Media" to reduce file size
  • Try importing directly through the app instead of share extension
  • Verify the chat export contains actual messages

App Crashes

If the app crashes:
  • Update to the latest version from the App Store
  • Restart your device
  • Try importing a smaller chat file
  • Contact support with crash details and device model
